# pageCast Marketing and Monetization Strategy

## Purpose

pageCast should sell multilingual story experiences directly on the pageCast platform, while TikTok, Shopee, Amazon, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, and other global channels act as lead generators.

The goal is not to sell downloadable files on marketplaces. The goal is to attract readers and parents, bring them into pageCast, register them as users, collect payment inside pageCast, and unlock the stories they paid for.

This gives pageCast ownership of:

- User accounts
- Email relationships
- Purchases
- Subscriptions
- Reading and listening history
- Retention and upsell opportunities

## Core Business Model

pageCast is a direct-to-consumer storytelling platform.

The product is not only a story. The product is:

- A screen-free bedtime routine
- An interactive audio story experience
- A safe story library for families
- A premium subscription for recurring story access
- A story world that can grow over time

The outside platforms create awareness. pageCast handles conversion, payment, access, and retention.

## Global Pricing and Language Policy

pageCast is a global platform.

Rules:

- All payments are priced and charged in USD.
- Stories can be published in many languages.
- Marketing can be localized by language and audience.
- The platform should display story language clearly.
- Do not tie the core business model to one country or local currency.

This lets pageCast sell globally while keeping the commerce system simple.

## Funnel Overview

Traffic source:

- TikTok videos
- Marketplace listings
- Amazon books or access pass listings
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts
- QR codes in printed or Kindle books

All traffic should point to:

- `/start`
- `/story/[slug]`
- `/pricing`
- `/signup`

The recommended user journey is:

1. User sees a story teaser or parent-focused post.
2. User clicks the link to pageCast.
3. User lands on a story page or campaign page.
4. User listens to Episode 1 for free.
5. User creates an account to continue.
6. User reaches a paywall for premium access.
7. User pays inside pageCast.
8. Story unlocks automatically.
9. User continues in their pageCast library.
10. pageCast upsells bundles or subscription later.

## Product Offers

### Free Preview

Every story should have a free first episode or preview scene.

Purpose:

- Reduce buyer hesitation
- Let users experience the player
- Capture interest before asking for payment

### Single Story Unlock

Example:

- `$3` to `$9` for one premium story

Best for:

- First-time buyers
- Marketplace leads
- TikTok impulse traffic
- Readers who only want one story

### Story Bundle

Example:

- `$9` to `$19` for 3 to 5 related stories

Recommended bundles:

- Kids Bedtime Pack
- Islamic Kids Stories Pack
- English Learning Stories Pack
- Multilingual Stories Pack
- Horror Story Pack
- Romance Story Pack

### Monthly Subscription

Example:

- `$9/month` to `$19/month` for Kids Unlimited

Includes:

- Unlimited premium kids stories
- New stories added monthly
- Cancel anytime
- Access from phone or tablet

This is the most important long-term revenue stream.

### Lifetime or Founding Member Pass

Example:

- `$29` to `$99` early supporter lifetime access

Use carefully as a launch offer, not as the default forever.

## Marketplaces

Shopee and similar marketplaces should be treated as trust and discovery channels, not the main payment system.

The marketplace product should not be positioned as a file download. Position it as a story access pass or a preview access guide that leads users back to pageCast.

Example product title:

> Kids Bedtime Audio Stories Access Pass - Screen-Free Stories Ages 4-8

What buyers receive:

- pageCast signup link
- Free story preview
- Access instructions
- Unlock code if the platform supports codes

Important note:

The preferred long-term flow is payment inside pageCast in USD. Marketplaces can still be useful for discovery, but the main business should push users to register and pay on pageCast.

Marketplace description template:

```text
What is this?
A calming audio story experience for children aged 4-8.

What you will receive:
- Link to create your free pageCast account
- Free Episode 1 preview
- Access instructions for premium stories
- Mobile-friendly listening experience

Why parents love it:
- Less screen time
- Helps build a bedtime routine
- Encourages imagination

Start here:
https://pagecast.app/start?utm_source=shopee
```

## Payment and Access Flow

The best flow is:

1. Visitor lands on pageCast.
2. Visitor listens to a free preview.
3. Visitor signs up before continuing.
4. Visitor hits the paywall.
5. Visitor chooses:
   - Buy this story
   - Subscribe monthly
6. Visitor pays through Stripe or local payment gateway.
7. Backend verifies the payment.
8. pageCast unlocks access.
9. Story appears in the user's library.

The front-end must never be the only access control. Playback and premium content access must be protected on the backend too.

## Access Control Logic

All premium playback should use one central rule:

```text
User can access a story if:
1. The story is free, or
2. The user purchased the story, or
3. The user has an active subscription.
```

This should power:

- Reader app playback
- Story page paywall
- User library
- Audio file access
- API routes that return premium story content

## UTM Tracking

Every external link should include a source.

Examples:

- `https://pagecast.app/start?utm_source=tiktok`
- `https://pagecast.app/start?utm_source=shopee`
- `https://pagecast.app/story/brave-little-camel?utm_source=amazon`
- `https://pagecast.app/story/brave-little-camel?utm_source=instagram`

On first visit:

1. Save `utm_source` in local storage or cookie.
2. Send it during signup.
3. Store it as `signup_source`.
4. Later connect revenue to source.

This lets pageCast answer:

- Which platform brings users?
- Which platform brings paying users?
- Which platform brings subscribers?
- Which stories convert best?
