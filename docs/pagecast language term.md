# PageCast Brand Language Guide

## Purpose

PageCast should not sound like a generic bookstore, audiobook app, or SaaS dashboard.

The product is not just a story or a book. A PageCast experience is read, heard, followed, unlocked, resumed, and enjoyed across scenes, voices, music, and moments.

This guide defines the official PageCast vocabulary, tone, and copywriting rules so the platform feels ownable, warm, magical, and consistent.

## Core Language System

### Platform

Official term: `PageCast`

Use for the product, company, and platform.

Example:

- Welcome to PageCast.
- PageCast brings Tales to life.

### Content Universe

Official term: `TaleVerse`

Use when talking about the full catalog or world of content.

Example:

- Explore the TaleVerse.
- New Tales are joining the TaleVerse soon.

### Emotional Marketing Word

Official term: `Tales`

Use in marketing, parent-facing copy, bedtime copy, and emotional storytelling.

Example:

- A world of Tales for curious minds.
- Bedtime Tales kids love and parents trust.

### Product Unit

Official term: `Cast`

A `Cast` is one complete PageCast experience.

Use `Cast` inside the app, pricing, library, checkout, creator tools, and access control copy.

Examples:

- Begin Cast
- Unlock Cast
- Resume Cast
- New Casts every week
- Your first Cast is ready

Do not use `book`, `audiobook`, or generic `story` as the main product noun in reader-facing UI unless clarity absolutely requires it.

## Content Structure

PageCast content should scale like this:

1. `Cast` - one complete PageCast experience
2. `Saga` - a collection or series of Casts
3. `Realm` - a larger world or franchise
4. `TaleVerse` - the full PageCast catalog

Examples:

- Begin this Cast.
- Follow the Luna Saga.
- Explore the Dragon School Realm.
- Discover new Casts in the TaleVerse.

## Monetization Terms

PageCast should avoid sounding like ecommerce.

| Avoid | Use Instead |
| --- | --- |
| Buy | Unlock |
| Purchase | Unlock |
| Add to cart | Unlock Cast |
| Subscription | Cast Pass |
| Free trial | Starter Pass |
| Premium | Premium Cast |
| Upgrade now | Get Cast Pass |
| Payment required | Unlock to continue |

Recommended plan language:

- `Starter Pass` - free previews and saved progress
- `Single Cast Unlock` - one-time Cast access
- `Cast Pass` - monthly access to premium Casts

Pricing remains in USD globally.

- Warm
- Calm
- Magical
- Encouraging
- Trustworthy
- Simple
- Parent-safe

PageCast is not:

- Corporate
- Pushy
- Tech-heavy
- Salesy
- Loud
- Overly cute

## Copywriting Rules

### 1. Use short, gentle sentences.

Avoid:

- Initiate playback of your selected content.

Use:

- Begin your Cast.

### 2. Be magical, not cheesy.

Avoid:

- The most epic adventure ever!!!

Use:

- A new Tale is waiting tonight.

### 3. Sell calm value, not pressure.

Avoid:

- Subscribe now before it is gone.

Use:

- Unlock more Tales whenever you are ready.

### 4. Use positive language.

Avoid:

- Do not miss out.

Use:

- Discover something new tonight.

### 5. Speak to kids and Guardians at the same time.

Kids should feel wonder. Guardians should feel trust.

Example:

- Cozy Tales for curious Explorers and calm nights for Guardians.
